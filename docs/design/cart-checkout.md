# Cart & Checkout — Inspiration Notes

> Reference only — not implementation.

## What to look for
- Cart pattern: slide-out drawer vs. dedicated page
- Checkout steps: single-page form vs. multi-step wizard (shipping → payment → review)
- Guest checkout visibility — is it the obvious default, or buried behind a forced login prompt?
- How shipping cost/taxes are shown before final payment (avoiding surprise costs is a major cause of cart abandonment)
- Order confirmation page: what it shows immediately vs. what's left to email
