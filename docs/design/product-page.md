## What to look for
- Image gallery pattern: thumbnails beside main image vs. below; zoom-on-hover vs. tap-to-expand
- Where price + "Add to cart" sit relative to scroll (sticky vs. static)
- Stock/urgency messaging ("only 3 left") — placement and tone
- Description layout: tabs (description/specs/reviews) vs. long scroll
